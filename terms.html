Messages are sent in response to inquiries you initiate. Frequency varies; expect approximately 1–5 messages per inquiry.

Message and data rates may apply. Jonathan Stuckey does not charge you for messages, but your mobile carrier may bill you according to your plan.

Opt out

You can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from Jonathan Stuckey. After you reply STOP, you will receive a single confirmation that you have been unsubscribed and we will not send you further messages unless you ask us to resume.
