Privacy Policy

Jonathan Stuckey · Last updated May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jonathan Stuckey ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information from people who contact us by phone, text message (SMS/MMS), or related channels.

Information we collect

  • Phone numbers used to call or text our business line.
  • Contents of voice calls, voicemails, and text messages you send to us.
  • Audio recordings and automated transcriptions of calls, where applicable, made for record-keeping and quality purposes.
  • Information you voluntarily share during a conversation (your name, callback details, the nature of your inquiry).

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To send operational follow-up messages directly related to your inquiry (for example, confirmations, status updates, or directions you asked for).
  • To maintain internal records of communications.

SMS / messaging

We send text messages only in response to inquiries you have initiated by contacting our business line, or as operational follow-ups directly related to those inquiries (such as appointment confirmations, order updates, delivery status, and similar transactional communications). We do not use your information to send marketing messages.

Phone number privacy: We do not share your phone number or message contents with third parties for marketing, promotional, or any non-operational purposes. Your phone number is used exclusively to deliver transactional messages related to services you have requested.

Message frequency: You will receive text messages only as needed for transactional updates and responses directly related to your inquiries. Frequency varies based on your needs and interactions.

Message and data rates: Standard message and data rates may apply. Check your mobile plan with your carrier for details.

Opt-out: You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP, OPTOUT, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, or REVOKE. Reply HELP for help.

How we share information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us deliver these communications (for example, our telephony and AI-services vendors), and only to the extent necessary to provide the service to you. We will disclose information when required by law.

Data retention

We retain call recordings, transcripts, and message contents for as long as needed for our records or as required by law, after which they are deleted.
